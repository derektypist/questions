General Questions on Lockdown

What Is A Lockdown?

A lockdown is the general cessation of activity, especially in response to a crisis.

What Must I Do In A Lockdown?

You must stay at home. However, you may leave your home for very limited purposes, such as exercising, shopping for food and seeking medical attention. You can travel to and from work, where it is not possible to work from home.

Can I Go On Holiday During Lockdown?

It is illegal to go on holiday during lockdown.
